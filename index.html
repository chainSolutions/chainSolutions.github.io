Founders

chainSolutions Founders

Simon Janin

Simon Janin studied Computer Science at ETH Zurich. He was one of the very first to see the potential of Smart Contracts and invest in Ethereum; since then, he got involved in several blockchain projects, most based on Ethereum.

Simon focuses on finding new ways of using cryptography to enable interactions that were not possible before. He's the inventor of the Filebounty protocol.

Dr. Arthur Gervais

Arthur obtained his PhD from ETH Zurich on the topic of blockchain security and is building a blockchain research group as Assistant Professor at Imperial College London.

Arthur is co-founder of a Smart Contract formal verification tool and co-author of the paper Do you need a Blockchain?. He has been involved in the blockchain space since 2011.
